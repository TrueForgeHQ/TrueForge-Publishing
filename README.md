# TrueForge Publishing

TrueForge Publishing is the publishing and collaboration arm of TrueForge Interactive.

We focus on bringing meaningful games and experiences to market while building partnerships with talented creators.

## Current Status

We are actively developing our first title. More information will be shared as development progresses.

## Collaborations

We are open to working with voice actors, 3D artists, writers, and other creative professionals.

If you're interested in potential collaboration opportunities, feel free to reach out.

## Long-term Vision

TrueForge Publishing aims to support a growing catalog of titles, explore tabletop publishing, and eventually expand into merchandise and community projects.
